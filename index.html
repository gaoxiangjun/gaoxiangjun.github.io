Xiangjun Gao

I am a second year PhD student at (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Long Quan. Before this, I obtained my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT).

My research focuses on 3D Computer Vision, including 3D Avatar, 3D Generation, and Editable Rendering. Previously, I interned at Microsoft Research, Tiktok, and Tencent AI Lab.
